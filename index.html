Yoasobi

Yoasobi is a Japanese music duo consisting of singer Ikura and composer Ayase. They are known for their unique and innovative music style, combining elements of pop, electronic, and alternative music to create touching and captivating songs. Yoasobi's lyrics often reflect human emotions and life experiences, making their music very relatable to listeners.
